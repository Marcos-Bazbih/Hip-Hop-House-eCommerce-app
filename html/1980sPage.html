The 1980's

The 80's is often referred to as the ‘golden age of hip-hop’. Run DMC, Public Enemy, N.W.A …all of these groups changed the face of rap/hip-hop forever. the Rap music was for the emergence of a new musical form. They gave the artists, who were formerly silenced, a chance to express themselves.
