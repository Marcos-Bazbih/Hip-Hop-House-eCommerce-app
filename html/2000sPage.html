The 2000's

When you think about hip-hop in the 2000’s you instantly think of the many ANTHEMS and CLASSICS the decade gave us. THE 2000’s ushered in a new age of artists who weren’t scared of TAKING RISKS and stepping out of hip-hops comfort zones. ​The 2000’s were A TIME TO BE ALIVE for the many ICONIC moments hip-hop gave us.
